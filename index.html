Abstract

In urban areas, as the growth of the population increases traffic becomes congested. In recent years there is an increase in usage of road vehicles and traffic also increased which is becoming a challenge for transportation system. In Present traffic system, it is not based on the traffic congestion level and a predefined time is allocated for traffic lights at every road crossing, which results in traffic congestion which causes the waiting at traffic junctions. This traffic congestion causes noise and air pollution. Moreover, it also causes fuel consumption. To overcome the above problem, the proposed system has the development of a congestion level-based traffic management system using IoT. It regulates the traffic light duration based on the real-time congestion level of the traffic measured at the road crossings by using ultrasonic sensors. The above Fig1 shows our Project Kit.
