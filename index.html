Traffic Congestion

In recent years, there islot of change in usage of vehicles which is becoming a challenge for transportation system. When predefined time is allocated at every road crossings which results in traffic congestion and becomes uncontrollable in peak traffic hours,which results the waiting at traffic junctions. To resolve the traffic congestion by construction of roads, improving the road infrastructure by construction of flyovers, bridges.However construction of roads in urban areas is impossible because of limited land resources. So, in recent years traffic congestion problem has solution by using of enabling technologies like Internet of Things (IOT).
